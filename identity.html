Accessibility

At CollaboCanvas, we are committed to making our platform accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

HTML Structure

We use semantic HTML to provide a clear and meaningful structure to our pages, making it easier for screen readers and other assistive technologies to navigate and understand the content.

Color Contrast

We have carefully chosen our color palette to ensure a sufficient contrast between foreground and background elements, meeting accessibility standards and making it easier for users with visual impairments to read and interact with the interface.

Keyboard Navigation

We have implemented keyboard navigation throughout our platform, allowing users to access all features and functions without the need for a mouse or other pointing device.

Screen Reader Compatibility

We have tested our platform with a variety of screen readers, and have made adjustments to ensure compatibility and optimize the experience for users who rely on this technology.
